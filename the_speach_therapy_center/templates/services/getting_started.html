{% endif %} My name is Sonya Tomanova, and I will be your speach therapist. By following the steps bellow, you can effectively get started with speech therapy sessions and work towards improving your communication skills.
Step 1: Initial Consultation:
  • Schedule an initial consultation with us. This may involve contacting the therapy center via phone, email, or our online contact form.
  • During the consultation, we will discuss your concerns, goals, and any relevant medical history regarding speech and language development.
Step 2: Assessment and Evaluation:
  • The speech therapist will conduct a comprehensive assessment to evaluate speech, language, and communication skills. This may include standardized tests, observations, and interviews.
  • Based on the assessment results, the therapist will identify areas of strength and areas that need improvement.
Step 3: Setting Goals:
  • Collaborate with the speech therapist to set specific and measurable goals for the therapy sessions. These goals should be tailored to address the individual needs and preferences of the client.
Step 4: Creating a Treatment Plan:
  • The therapist will develop a customized treatment plan outlining the strategies and techniques that will be used to address the identified speech and language goals.
  • The treatment plan may include a variety of activities, exercises, and interventions designed to improve communication skills.
Step 5: Therapy Sessions:
  • Attend regular therapy sessions as scheduled. The frequency and duration of sessions may vary depending on the individual's needs and the therapist's recommendations.
  • During each session, the therapist will guide the client through targeted activities and exercises to work towards achieving the established goals.
Step 6: Practice and Homework:
  • Practice the skills and techniques learned during therapy sessions in everyday situations.
  • The therapist may provide homework assignments or activities to reinforce learning and facilitate progress between sessions.
Step 7: Progress Monitoring and Adjustments:
  • Regularly monitor progress towards the established goals through ongoing assessments and evaluations.
  • Based on progress, the therapist may adjust the treatment plan as needed to ensure continued improvement and success.
Step 8: Continued Support and Follow-Up:
  • Maintain open communication with the speech therapist to address any concerns or questions that may arise during the therapy process.
  • Follow up with the therapist as recommended to track progress and receive additional support as needed.
